## üîß How to Change Themes:

Simply replace `theme=default` with your chosen theme in all the widget URLs in your README.md:

**Example:**
```markdown
# Change from:
theme=default

# To:
theme=tokyonight
```

You can paste these URLs directly into your browser to preview how they'll look!
